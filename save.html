A quick note before your trip

Upgrades and perks depend on availability and hotel discretion. This guidance improves odds, but it can't guarantee outcomes.

The best results come from polite, specific requests and good timing.

Do
  • Ask early when possible (24–36 hours before arrival)
  • Be specific and flexible
  • Keep the tone calm and professional
Avoid
  • Entitled wording
  • Asking during peak check-in rush
  • Assuming upgrades are owed
